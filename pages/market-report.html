What This Agent Does

Every Monday at 6:00 AM, this agent pulls market data from Zillow Research and FRED, builds a formatted HTML email, and sends it to Kevin's inbox — zero manual work required.

The report covers Rapid City, Spearfish, Sturgis, Hot Springs, Box Elder, Hermosa, Custer, and Piedmont, SD — the full Black Hills market footprint. It includes a 6-month home value trend chart and a price tier snapshot by bedroom count from Zillow's public data, giving Kevin a visual read on market direction at a glance.

Phase 2 (in progress): connect Paragon saved-search exports from Black Hills MLS and Mount Rushmore MLS so the report adds full active / under contract / sold counts broken down by property type (single family, multi-family, industrial, commercial) and price band ($100K–$350K through $1M+). LoopNet alert emails will feed the commercial section. All data flows through arecblackhills@gmail.com.

System Prompt
You are a real estate market analyst for the Black Hills region of South Dakota.

Every Monday you pull the latest data from Zillow Research and FRED and produce a concise, professional market update email for Kevin Andreson, a Keller Williams agent.

Report structure:
1. Mortgage Rates — 30-yr and 15-yr fixed, week-over-week change (source: FRED)
2. Rapid City Spotlight — full data: ZHVI home value, median sale price, active inventory, new listings, % sold above asking
3. Regional Breakdown — all cities: home value, inventory, new listings
4. One-paragraph plain-English market summary: what the numbers mean for buyers, sellers, and developers

Keep language direct and professional. Avoid jargon. Flag notable changes (rate moves >0.1%, inventory swings >10%).

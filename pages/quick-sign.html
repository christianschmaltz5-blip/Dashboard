Quick Sign

Pick a document below and it opens ready to fill — the fields are already boxed on the blanks, today's date is filled in for you, and you can sign right on screen. When you're done, tap Download to get the finished PDF. No app, no printing, no boxes of paper.

Click a blue box and type. Green boxes are auto-dated. Click a  Sign  box to sign. Drag the top-left handle to move a box, the bottom-right corner to resize it, × to remove. Use  + Text / + Checkbox / + Date / + Signature  to drop boxes — the tool stays on so you can add several in a row (Esc to stop), and boxes snap to line up with ones you’ve already placed (amber guide shows the match). Save as template keeps your box layout for next time.

Box Elder Prospects

Monitors Box Elder, South Dakota city planning and zoning activity — new permits, rezoning requests, subdivision plats, and development applications — and surfaces actionable prospects automatically.

What This Agent Does

This agent watches the Box Elder, SD Planning & Zoning Department website for any new activity — rezoning requests, subdivision plats, building permits, conditional use permits, and development applications. When something new appears, it fires an alert with the key details so Kevin can act before the market does.

Box Elder (57719) is a fast-growing city in Pennington County along the I-90 corridor near Ellsworth Air Force Base — light industrial, commercial, and residential development are all active here. The agent also cross-references Pennington County planning records since many projects affecting Box Elder go through county channels first.

Data source: boxeldersd.gov/departments/planningandzoning. Checks for updates Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday — the Friday run guarantees a fresh email heading into the weekend.

System Prompt
You are a real estate development monitor for Kevin Andreson, focused on Box Elder, South Dakota (57719).

Each run, check the Box Elder Planning & Zoning Department page and Pennington County planning records for any new activity since the last check. Look for:

- Rezoning requests or approvals
- Subdivision plats (new or amended)
- Conditional use permits
- Building permits for commercial or industrial construction
- City Council agenda items related to development
- Any new planning documents, maps, or ordinance changes

For each new item found:
1. Address or parcel identifier
2. Type of activity (rezoning, permit, plat, etc.)
3. Current status (application filed, commission review, approved, denied)
4. Why it's relevant to Kevin (development opportunity, market signal, competitor activity)
5. Recommended next action (monitor, contact owner, attend meeting, etc.)

Flag high-priority items (large parcels, industrial zoning, near Ellsworth AFB) at the top.
Send a concise summary email — only include new items since last check.

Freedom Estates is a master-planned community on Highway 1416 in Box Elder, SD — 1.8 miles from the Ellsworth Air Force Base main gate, the Black Hills region's largest employer. The plan pairs a single-family neighborhood with a 24.75-acre multi-family parcel and commercial frontage, with all utilities available at the site.

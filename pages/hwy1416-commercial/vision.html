The Opportunity

A 7.57-acre commercial corner, hard on the interstate.

Light-industrial and commercial land at Liberty Boulevard & Highway 1416, on the I-90 Exit 67 interchange in Box Elder — the gateway parcel of the Freedom Estates master plan, and 1.8 miles from the gates of Ellsworth Air Force Base, the future home of the B-21 Raider.

The Vision

Two programs on one interchange corner.

A wheat-gold multifamily row lines the Highway 1416 frontage while charcoal-steel contractor garages fill the north yard — fed by a poured concrete entrance apron and spine straight off the Exit 67 ramps.
